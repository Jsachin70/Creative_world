      Vidant Health

      Welcome to Apollo Health. We are a regional health system serving 29 counties in eastern North Carolina,
      and we are working every day to improve the health of the 1.4 million people we serve.
      Apollo Health is made up of eight hospitals, physician practices, home health, hospice,
      wellness centers and other health care services. The hospitals are:

    • Appollo Beaufort Hospital, Washington
    • Bertie Hospital, Windsor
    • Chowan Hospital, Edenton
    • Appollo Duplin Hospital, Kenansville
    • Apollo Edgecombe Hospital, Tarboro
    • Apollo Chowan Hospital, Edenton
    • Apollo Medical Center, Greenville
    • Apollo Roanoke-Chowan Hospital, Ahoskie
    • The Outer Banks Hospital, Nags Head (jointly owned with Chesapeake Regional Medical Center)


      • Apollo Medical Group is a reputable and established multi-specialty physician group
      that provides superior care for the health and wellness needs of eastern North Carolina's patients.hello
      With more than 350 primary and specialty care providers in more than 70 locations, quality health care is never far from home.

      Apollo Medical Center is affiliated with the Brody School of Medicine at East Carolina University.

      As a major resource for health services and education, Vidant Health strives to support local medical communities
      and to work with providers throughout the region to deliver quality care.

      Our goal is to enhance services that are available locally and to eliminate barriers involving time,
      distance and lack of awareness that sometimes prevent patients from receiving the care they need.
