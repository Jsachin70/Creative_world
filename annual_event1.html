      JUNE 2016

      JUNE 21

       7th Annual Community Faith Leaders' Seminar

      June 21, 2016, 8:00am-11Pm
      Contact :
      Contact Information : 1-800-472-8500
      Location: Location: Edwin W. Monroe AHEC Conference Center, 2000 Venture Tower Drive, Greenville
      Cost : $ FREE
      Sponsor : The Mildred Sheffield Wells Charitable Trust and The James J. and Mamie Richardson Perkins Trust


      Join us for "Sabbath Keeping: Tending the Garden of Our Minds, Bodies and Souls.
" The goal of this seminar is to provide an opportunity for faith leaders 
to develop and put into practice a new perspective and rhythm
 for self-care and well-being. This experience will help you recognize 
 that it is difficult to offer genuine pastoral care and love to others 
 and to invite them to love themselves when we continuously exempt ourselves 
 from this same caring. In exploring sabbath keeping, you will be guided along 
 a continuum of recognizing the gift to give care. God also calls and requires 
 you to embrace the gift of taking care. It is the hope that you walk away from 
 this seminar with tools to continuously engage this practice. 
Registration begins at 8:00 am. Sessions will run from 9:00 am until noon.
Lunch will be served at noon. Our speaker is the Rev. Dr. Betty M. Lovelace-Ross. 
She has provided leadership development to many faith-based, educational, 
law enforcement and corporate clients for more than 30 years.

Please register by June 20 to ensure your meal and seminar materials.
